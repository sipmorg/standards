
.Foreword
The Society for Integrative Phytomedicine (SIPM) is an international organization
dedicated to the development of standards for phytomedicine, medicinal fungi,
and related natural health products. The work of preparing SIPM Standards is
carried out through technical committees composed of experts from academia,
industry, regulatory bodies, and healthcare practice.

The procedures used to develop this document and those intended for its further
maintenance are described in the SIPM Directives, Part 1. In particular, the
different approval criteria needed for the different types of SIPM documents
should be noted. This document was drafted in accordance with the editorial
rules of the SIPM Directives, Part 2.
