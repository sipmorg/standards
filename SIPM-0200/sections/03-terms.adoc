[[terms]]
== Terms and definitions

For the purposes of this document, the following terms and definitions apply.
Terms defined in SIPM-0001 also apply where relevant.

=== cultivation site

area of land or controlled environment used for growing medicinal plants

NOTE: Cultivation sites may include open fields, greenhouses, hydroponic
systems, or other controlled environments.

=== seed stock

material used for propagation, including true seeds, cuttings, rhizomes,
bulbs, or other vegetative propagation material

=== cultivation protocol

documented procedures for growing a specific medicinal plant species,
including environmental parameters, inputs, and timing

=== plant protection

measures taken to protect medicinal plants from pests, diseases, and other
threats to their health and quality

=== primary processing

initial processing of plant material after harvest, including cleaning,
drying, cutting, and packaging

NOTE: Primary processing does not include extraction or formulation.

=== batch

defined quantity of plant material produced in a single process or series of
processes, allowing for traceability

=== traceability

ability to trace the history, application, or location of plant material
through recorded identification data
